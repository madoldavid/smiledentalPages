Guidelines

Make the most of your visit.

Arrival

Please arrive 5–10 minutes early. Late arrivals over 10 minutes may be rebooked to keep on schedule.

Cancellations

Free up to 24 hours before. Within 24 hours a £30 fee may apply for non-emergency changes.

Urgent issues

Select “Emergency” in notes and call us after booking; we hold daily urgent slots.

Preparation

Take regular meds as normal unless advised. Avoid heavy meals 1 hour before hygiene/whitening.
